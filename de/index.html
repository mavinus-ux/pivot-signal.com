An Expert Advisor (EA) is an automated trading program that runs on the MetaTrader 5 platform. It uses pre-programmed trading rules, technical indicators, and algorithms to identify setups and execute trades on your behalf — without manual intervention. Our EAs (Pivot Point, Breakout, Trendfolge) cover the three core trading strategies: mean-reversion at pivot levels, range breakout detection, and multi-timeframe trend following. Once installed, you can "set and forget" — the EA scans the markets 24/5, identifies high-probability setups, manages entries, sets stop-loss and take-profit levels, and exits trades according to strict, backtested rules.
